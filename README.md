# CocoaCountyMap

Election night deserves more than spreadsheets and static PDFs.

CocoaCountyMap turns complex results into a clear, interactive experience that helps voters, media, and community leaders understand what happened at a glance.

Try it here: [Live Demo](https://cocoa-county.github.io/CocoaCountyMap/public/)

## Features

- Interactive precinct-level election results map
- Multiple views for winners, turnout, and candidate performance
- Guided tour for first-time visitors
- Responsive design for desktop, tablet, and mobile
- Clean interface built for public-facing election reporting

## Why People Love It

- Easy to explore: Click any precinct and instantly see key results.
- Built for trust: Present data in a transparent, visual format people can follow.
- Mobile friendly: Works smoothly across desktop, tablet, and phone.
- Fast to understand: Clear views for winners, turnout, and candidate performance.
- Great first impression: Modern interface with a guided tour for new visitors.

## What It Delivers

CocoaCountyMap helps organizations communicate election outcomes with confidence.

- Share precinct-level insights without overwhelming your audience.
- Make local trends and turnout patterns easy to spot.
- Give the public a polished, professional destination for results.
- Support press, campaigns, and community partners with a map they can actually use.

## Perfect For

- County election offices
- Newsrooms and civic organizations
- Campaign teams and policy groups
- Anyone who needs to present election results clearly and credibly
